Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 May 2026

Deadlinely (“we”, “our”) is operated by GateX. This policy explains how we handle information when you use the Deadlinely iOS app and this website (deadlinely.gatex.uk).

Summary

  • Your deadlines are stored on your device using Apple’s SwiftData.
  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • Subscriptions are processed by Apple; purchase status may be checked via RevenueCat.
  • Notifications are scheduled locally on your device when you enable them.

Information we collect

Data you provide: deadline titles, dates, reminder preferences, and optional settings you enter in the app. This data stays on your iPhone unless you delete it or remove the app.

Subscription data: if you subscribe to Deadlinely Pro, Apple processes payment. We receive subscription status through RevenueCat to unlock Pro features. We do not receive your full payment card details.

Support email: if you contact us at app-care@gatex.uk, we receive the information you choose to send (such as your email address and message content).

Website: this static site does not use analytics cookies. Standard server logs from our host may include IP address, browser type, and pages requested.

How we use information

  • Provide countdowns, widgets, and reminders
  • Validate Pro access and restore purchases
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve stability and fix bugs when you report issues

Sharing

We share data only with service providers needed to run the app:

  • Apple — App Store, in-app purchases, and on-device storage
  • RevenueCat — subscription and entitlement management

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and users.

Retention

Deadline data remains on your device until you delete it or use “Delete local data” in Settings. Support emails are kept only as long as needed to resolve your request.

Your choices

  • Delete deadlines or all local data in the app
  • Turn off notifications in iOS Settings
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions in Apple ID → Subscriptions

Children

Deadlinely is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

International users

If you use the app from outside the United Kingdom, your information may be processed where our providers operate. By using Deadlinely you consent to this processing.

Changes

We may update this policy. We will post the new date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about privacy: app-care@gatex.uk
