Common questions

How do I add a widget?

Long-press your Home Screen or Lock Screen → Add Widget → search for Deadlinely. The in-app widget tutorial walks you through it after onboarding.

How do reminders work?

When creating or editing a deadline, enable reminders for the day before and/or the morning of the due date. Allow notifications when iOS prompts you.

Restore purchases / Deadlinely Pro

Open Settings in the app → Restore purchases. Subscriptions are tied to your Apple ID. Manage billing in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Delete my data

Settings → Delete local data removes all deadlines and reminders on this device and returns you to the welcome flow. This does not cancel your App Store subscription.

Report a bug

Email app-care@gatex.uk with your iOS version, device model, and steps to reproduce. Screenshots help.
